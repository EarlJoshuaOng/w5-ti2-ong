Red

Red is the first color of the rainbow, it is one of the primary colors and it symbolizes passion, love, and anger, and it has the lowest frequency in the light spectrum.

Orange

Orange is the second color of the rainbow and the a name of a fruit. It represents youth, energy, and happiness. Orange may be created by mixing red and yellow.

Yellow

Yellow is the brightest color in the spectrum and is one of the three primary colors. It symbolizes happiness, hope, and deceit.

Green

Green is the most soothing color and it is the midmost color of the spectrum. Green is the color of nature, new beginnings, and abundance. Mixing blue and yellow creates green.

Blue

Blue is for calm, responsible, and sadness. It is the color of the sea and is another primary color alongside red and yellow.

Indigo

Indigo is a color made by mixing violet and blue. It means tranquility, harmony, confidence, and integrity.

Violet

Violet represents royalty, power, and ambition, it is the color that has the highest frequency in the spectrum. Don't forget! It is the last color of the rainbow.
